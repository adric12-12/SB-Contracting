About us

At SB Contracting, we are passionate about providing innovative, high-quality solutions in the welding and insulation fields. Founded in 2023, our company has grown thanks to our attention to detail, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Our mission

To provide top-tier welding and insulation services that contribute to the efficiency and safety of our clients, all while maintaining the highest standards of quality and responsibility.

Our services

Ship and shipyard maintenance

Involves inspections, repairs, and upgrades to ensure vessels' safety, efficiency, and longevity, as well as the upkeep of shipyard facilities.

Welding

Ensure structural integrity through specialized techniques for hulls, decks, and internal structures, providing strength and watertightness.

Insulation

Enhance thermal, acoustic, and fire protection by applying specialized materials to hulls, decks, and interiors, ensuring efficiency and safety.
